Straight talk: how running depth really works

Real running depth comes down to your lure, your line diameter, your speed, and how much line you've got out. But it also depends on things no app can fully see: current, water density, line wear, even how a specific lure was tuned at the factory.

Because of that, no app can predict depth to 100% accuracy — anyone claiming it is selling something. Depth Vector's physics engine is built and verified against controlled on-water testing to be as close as physics allows. That's the honest ceiling, and we work right up against it.

Coming in a future release Tuning: when you know your true depth — from your electronics or bottom contact — you'll be able to tell the app, and it will adjust predictions for your boat and your gear. This is not in the app today; when it ships, your numbers get personal.
